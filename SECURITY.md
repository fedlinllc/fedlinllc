# Security Policy

## Supported Versions

We actively support security updates for all our service repositories. Please check individual repository documentation for version-specific support information.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in any of our repositories or services, please report it responsibly:

**Email:** security@fedlin.com

Please include:
- Description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce (if applicable)
- Potential impact
- Suggested fix (if you have one)

We will acknowledge receipt of your report within 48 hours and provide an initial assessment within 7 business days.

## Security Best Practices

As a security consultancy, we follow industry best practices:

- **Customer-tenant-first architecture** - Evidence and logs remain in customer environments
- **OIDC-based authentication** - No long-lived credentials in our delivery pipelines
- **Infrastructure as Code** - All changes are version-controlled and auditable
- **Policy as Code** - Security controls are defined declaratively

## Disclosure Policy

- We will work with you to understand and resolve the issue quickly
- We will keep you informed of our progress
- We will credit you for the discovery (unless you prefer to remain anonymous)
- We will not take legal action against security researchers acting in good faith

Thank you for helping keep FEDLIN and our customers secure.
